When calculating Ontario payroll deductions (CPP, CPP2, EI, RRSP, etc.), always start from pay stub actuals and calculate per-paycheck drawdown against remaining annual balances.

**Why:** Annualized or projected deduction rates diverge from reality mid-year, especially during job transitions. Zee has had detailed modeling work done on this and relies on per-paycheck accuracy.

**How to apply:** When Zee provides pay stub data, use those actual per-check amounts. Calculate remaining annual room by subtracting YTD actuals from annual limits/maximums. Never substitute CRA published annual rates for known per-check actuals when the actuals are available.
