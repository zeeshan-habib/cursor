**Core silhouette rules:**
- Vertical lines always — monochromatic dressing elongates
- Dark bottoms always — light/mid-tone pants widen and shorten
- Slim-straight fit only — no skinny, relaxed, or wide-leg
- Shoulder seams must land exactly at shoulder edge
- Shirts untucked at mid-fly (not thigh)
- Structured outerwear only — topcoats/overcoats, never waist-length

**Best colours (ranked):**
1. Black, charcoal, dark navy — foundation
2. Burgundy/deep wine — most flattering on complexion
3. Olive green, forest green, deep teal
4. Rust, terracotta, mustard
5. Plum, cobalt, royal blue

**Key wardrobe gaps to fill:**
1. Burgundy/wine merino crewneck — Uniqlo Extra Fine Merino ~$50-60
2. Charcoal slim-straight chinos — Uniqlo Smart Ankle Pants ~$50
3. Structured topcoat/overshirt jacket in charcoal or dark navy ~$80-130

**Pieces doing damage:**

- Maroon bomber — waist-length, cuts at widest point
- Light purple long-sleeve tee — fights warm complexion
