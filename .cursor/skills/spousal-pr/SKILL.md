## RELATIONSHIP EVIDENCE — OUTLAND COUPLE

Long-distance couples often lack joint lease/bank accounts. Compensate with:

| Category | What to include |
|---|---|
| Communication | Dated chat/call logs spanning the relationship — not just recent months |
| Visits | Boarding passes, visas, stamps, photos from each visit |
| Wedding | Certificate, photos, invitations, affidavits from family |
| Financial | Remittance records, gifts, shared trip expenses |
| Third parties | Statutory declarations from people who know you as a couple |

Flag weak spots: gaps in timeline, inconsistent names/dates across forms, missing translations.
