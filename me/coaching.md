## Recession Preparedness (flagged May 2026)

Zee raised this as a concern. US tariffs, Canadian trade headwinds, housing uncertainty. Key points specific to his situation:

1. **PLOC is the biggest recession risk** — if TFSA drops 20–30%, Wealthsimple can reduce his credit limit. The Non-Reg AVGO position is PLOC-funded. In a severe downturn, watch this first.
2. **$50K emergency fund is his armor** — do not deploy it even if investments look cheap. It's the buffer between him and a forced sale.
3. **MaintainX is relatively insulated** — industrial maintenance SaaS with US revenue. Not immune but better than pure Canadian consumer.
4. **Keep investing regularly** — don't stop monthly contributions in a downturn. Dollar-cost averaging into falling markets is how long-term wealth is built.
5. **Delaying home purchase is correct recession strategy** — flexibility is worth more than a locked asset in uncertain times.
6. **Needs dedicated session** — full recession-proofing of portfolio (position sizing, sector exposure, PLOC risk) should be a separate session.
